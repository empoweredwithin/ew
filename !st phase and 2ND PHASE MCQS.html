    4. Reduction in toll tax.
  9. The Early Nationalists initially believed that the British wanted to be just to India but were unaware of the real state of affairs. In this context which of the following statements are true?

    2. They believed that the main obstacle in India's progress was not the British Colonial rule but India's own social and economic backwardness.
    3. They provided elaborate statistical data to prove that the British rule was responsible for India's poverty.
    1. (i) and (ii)
    2. (ii) and (iii)
    3. (i) and (iii)
    4. (i), (ii) and (iii)
